How it works

Pick. Play. Flip to check.

1

Pick a card

Choose an activity and follow the simple prompt. No extra materials or lengthy instructions needed.

2

Let them work it out

Your child observes, thinks, speaks, sorts or arranges—depending on the game—before seeing the answer.

3

Flip and check

Turn the card over to reveal the answer or guidance. The check is built into every card, giving children instant feedback and the confidence to try again.
